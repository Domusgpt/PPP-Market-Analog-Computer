# Geometric Information Theory: Mathematical foundations for a unified framework

The 24-cell polytope exhibits unique mathematical properties that position it as a candidate "generative kernel" for information-theoretic structure. Its tripartite decomposition into three interlocking 16-cells corresponds precisely to the index-3 subgroup relationship W(D₄) ⊂ W(F₄), while the golden ratio (φ) mediates its connection to the 120-cell and 600-cell through Gosset's construction. This report establishes rigorous mathematical, philosophical, and physical foundations for a proposed Geometric Information Theory (GIT) that synthesizes process metaphysics, information physics, and polytope mathematics into a framework capable of addressing consciousness and emergence.

## The 24-cell as generative kernel: mathematical structure

The 24-cell occupies a singular position among regular polytopes—it is the only convex regular 4-polytope without a three-dimensional analog. Its **24 vertices** admit two equivalent coordinate representations: the 8 permutations of (±1, 0, 0, 0) combined with the 16 vertices (±½, ±½, ±½, ±½), or equivalently, all permutations of (±1, ±1, 0, 0) at radius √2. When interpreted as quaternions, these vertices constitute the **24 unit Hurwitz quaternions**, forming the binary tetrahedral group 2T of order 24.

The critical mathematical property for GIT is the **tripartite decomposition theorem**: the 24 vertices partition into three disjoint sets of 8 vertices, each defining a regular 16-cell. Specifically, the 8 integer vertices (±1, 0, 0, 0) form one 16-cell, while the 16 half-integer vertices divide according to parity—those with an even number of minus signs forming a second 16-cell, and those with odd parity forming the third. This decomposition reflects the group-theoretic structure where W(D₄) is a subgroup of index 3 within W(F₄), the full symmetry group of the 24-cell (order **1,152**).

The index-3 relationship is not merely combinatorial but connected to the **triality phenomenon** unique to the D₄ Lie algebra. The Dynkin diagram of D₄ possesses S₃ symmetry—the only simple Lie algebra whose outer automorphism group includes order-3 elements. This triality permutes three 8-dimensional irreducible representations of Spin(8): the vector representation V, positive spinor S⁺, and negative spinor S⁻. Geometrically, triality permutes the three inscribed 16-cells of the 24-cell, providing mathematical precision to the proposed Alpha-Beta-Gamma structure.

## Golden ratio generation of higher polytopes

The connection between the 24-cell and the φ-symmetric polytopes (120-cell and 600-cell) is mediated by explicit geometric constructions involving the golden ratio φ = (1+√5)/2. The **600-cell's 120 vertices** (with circumradius 2) include the 24 vertices (±2, 0, 0, 0) forming an inscribed 24-cell, plus 96 additional vertices whose coordinates involve φ, 1/φ, and their combinations through even permutations.

**Gosset's construction** provides the generative mechanism: the 600-cell emerges from the 24-cell by partitioning each edge at the golden ratio in a coherent manner determined by the tripartite vertex coloring. The procedure assigns each 24-cell vertex a color (corresponding to its 16-cell membership), establishes a cyclic order on each triangular face, and divides edges at golden sections according to direction. The result yields the snub 24-cell (96 vertices), which combined with the original 24 vertices produces the 600-cell's vertex configuration.

Mathematically, this relationship manifests in the **H₄ root system decomposition**:

> H₄ = 24-cell ∪ snub 24-cell

under the symmetry group (W(D₄)/C₂):S₃ of order 576. The **120 icosians**—unit quaternions forming the binary icosahedral group 2I—constitute the vertices of a 600-cell and establish the non-crystallographic H₄ Coxeter group with order **14,400**. The appearance of φ throughout H₄ coordinate formulas reflects the icosahedral symmetry's deep connection to the golden ratio.

Perhaps most remarkably, the **E₈ root system** (240 vectors, relevant to string theory and mathematical physics) decomposes as:

> E₈ = H₄ ⊕ φ·H₄

Two copies of H₄, scaled by φ relative to each other, constitute the exceptional root system through a 4×8 projection matrix. This establishes a precise mathematical sense in which golden ratio scaling "generates" higher structures from the 24-cell foundation.

## Process philosophy provides the metaphysical architecture

Alfred North Whitehead's process metaphysics supplies conceptual resources remarkably aligned with GIT's geometric framework. His **actual occasions** are momentary events of experience constituting reality's ultimate furniture—"the final real things of which the world is made up" (*Process and Reality*, p. 18). Each occasion exhibits a **dipolar structure**: a physical pole inheriting from past occasions through causal influence, and a mental pole conceptually prehending eternal objects (pure potentials for determination).

The central process concept is **prehension**—derived from "apprehension" minus cognitive connotations—which describes how occasions "grasp" or "feel" their world. Crucially, prehension has **triadic structure**: (1) the subject (concrescing occasion), (2) the datum (object prehended), and (3) the subjective form (the "how" of prehension—emotional, evaluative character). This triadic architecture parallels GIT's proposal of Alpha-Beta-Gamma 16-cells as dialectical poles and emerging prototype.

Whitehead's **concrescence** (*concrescere*: "to grow together") describes how an actual occasion achieves determinacy through successive phases: initial reception of data, supplemental integration with conceptual feelings, aesthetic synthesis of contrasts, and final "satisfaction" as determinate unity. His formula for creativity—"**the many become one and are increased by one**"—captures the integrative drive from multiplicity to unity that GIT proposes as geometrically fundamental.

The **subject-superject** structure exhibits dialectical character: each occasion is simultaneously subject (active process of becoming) and superject (completed achievement providing datum for successor occasions). This is not Hegelian dialectic as purely conceptual development but **real dialectic**—the opposition and unity of becoming and being within concrete process. Future exercises anticipatory influence on present through the "subjective aim."

**Eternal objects**—Whitehead's reformed Platonic forms—include "eternal objects of the objective species" which are explicitly "the mathematical Platonic forms." Geometric structures, numerical relations, and proportions constitute genuine eternal objects that achieve concrete realization through "ingression" into actual occasions. The extensive continuum provides proto-geometric structure underlying metric space, while the method of extensive abstraction derives geometric entities (points, lines, planes) from concrete extensive relations rather than assuming them as primitive.

Henri Bergson's complementary concept of **duration** (*durée*) emphasizes the qualitative, indivisible, irreversible character of lived time that resists spatialization. His critique warns against treating time like space—the "cinematographic mechanism" whereby intellect takes snapshots and artificially reconstructs movement. This caution applies to GIT: geometric structure must capture process's temporal asymmetry rather than merely providing static spatial models.

## Information physics: geometry from information structure

Contemporary physics increasingly suggests intimate connections between information and geometry. The **Bekenstein bound** provides the foundational result: the maximum entropy (information) containable within a region is proportional to its surface area, not volume:

> S ≤ 2πkRE/ℏc

where R is radius, E is energy. The **Bekenstein-Hawking entropy** S_BH = kA/4ℓ_p² scaling with event horizon area motivated Gerard 't Hooft and Leonard Susskind's **holographic principle**: "the description of a volume of space can be thought of as encoded on a lower-dimensional boundary." Ted Jacobson's remarkable derivation (1995) showed Einstein's field equations follow from assuming Bekenstein bounds plus thermodynamic laws—suggesting gravity **emerges** from information-theoretic constraints.

John Archibald Wheeler's "**it from bit**" thesis proposes that "every item of the physical world has at bottom...an immaterial source and explanation; that what we call reality arises in the last analysis from the posing of yes-no questions and the registering of equipment-evoked responses." His participatory universe places observer-participation as fundamental to physics, though the mathematical formalization remains incomplete.

The contemporary "**it from qubit**" program, exemplified by the **ER=EPR conjecture** (Maldacena-Susskind, 2013), proposes that every entangled EPR pair of particles is connected by an Einstein-Rosen bridge (wormhole). Within AdS/CFT correspondence, the thermofield double state—representing two entangled CFTs—is dual to an eternal black hole with geometric ER bridge. Recent work provides "concrete and computable realization" of ER=EPR, identifying Bekenstein-Hawking entropy with entanglement entropy between subsystems.

**Tensor network** models (particularly MERA—Multiscale Entanglement Renormalization Ansatz) provide explicit constructions where geometric structure emerges from entanglement patterns. Brian Swingle's proposed "AdS/MERA correspondence" shows tensor networks with hyperbolic geometry resembling AdS spatial slices, with the Ryu-Takayanagi formula (relating boundary entanglement entropy to bulk minimal surface area) emerging naturally from bond index counting.

**Causal set theory** (Sorkin et al.) proposes spacetime is fundamentally discrete—a locally finite partially ordered set where "**order + number = geometry**." The partial order encodes causal structure; counting elements gives volume. Malament's theorem establishes that causal structure plus conformal factor determines full spacetime geometry. Similarly, **loop quantum gravity** quantizes geometry directly, with area operator having discrete spectrum: A = 8πγℓ_p² Σᵢ √(jᵢ(jᵢ+1)), where spin labels j index discrete area quanta.

The quantum state space itself has natural geometric structure via the **Fubini-Study metric** on projective Hilbert space CP^n. The quantum geometric tensor (QGT) has real part giving quantum distance and imaginary part giving Berry curvature—experimentally measurable quantities (demonstrated in nitrogen-vacancy centers, 2021) establishing that geometry is intrinsic to quantum information.

## IIT 4.0 and the geometry of consciousness

Integrated Information Theory (IIT) 4.0, formalized by Albantakis et al. (2023), provides the most developed mathematical framework representing consciousness as geometric structure. Beginning from phenomenological axioms (existence, intrinsicality, information, integration, exclusion, composition), IIT derives physical postulates that substrates of consciousness must satisfy, expressed through rigorous mathematical formalism.

The central construct is the **Φ-structure**—a geometric object in "qualia space" Q where each axis represents a possible system state. The theory's identity claim: **an experience IS identical to a cause-effect structure unfolded from a maximal substrate**. This structure consists of:

- **Distinctions**: Cause-effect states specified by mechanism-purview pairs, represented as points in Q with associated integrated information φ^d
- **Relations**: Congruent overlaps among distinctions, connecting points in Q with associated φ^r values  
- **Structure Integrated Information (Φ)**: Sum of all distinction and relation φ values

The "constellation metaphor" describes how concepts form a "constellation of stars" in qualia space—the complete geometric "shape" specifying the quality of experience. As Tononi writes: "The shape of the constellation of concepts in qualia space completely specifies the quality of a particular experience and distinguishes it from other experiences."

The mathematical formalism involves:

1. **Transition Probability Matrix (TPM)**: T_U ≡ p(ū|u) operationally defining the substrate
2. **Intrinsic Information (ii)**: Product of selectivity and informativeness measuring difference a system makes over itself
3. **Integrated Information (φ)**: Irreducibility over minimum information partition (MIP)
4. **Intrinsic Difference (ID)**: Uniquely satisfying causality, intrinsicality, and specificity requirements

Gärdenfors' **conceptual spaces theory** provides complementary geometric semantics: concepts are convex regions in quality dimension spaces, with prototypes as central points, typicality decreasing with distance, and conceptual combination as geometric operations (intersection, mapping). Neural population geometry research demonstrates that neural state spaces exhibit low-dimensional manifold structure where representations are "untangled" through processing, connecting theoretical frameworks to empirical neuroscience.

## Formalizing emergence through category theory

Category-theoretic approaches provide rigorous mathematical formalization for emergence. The core criterion, developed by Adam (2017) and La Guardia & Miranda (2018): **emergence occurs when a representing functor fails to preserve colimits**.

**Definition (Colimit):** For a diagram F: J → C, a colimit is a co-cone (L, φ_X) universal among co-cones—representing the "synthesis" of diagram components.

**Definition (Emergence):** A functor Φ: C → D exhibits emergent effects if and only if Φ is not co-continuous (fails to preserve some colimit).

For Abelian categories, this criterion admits homological formulation: Φ sustains emergent effects iff for some exact sequence A → A' → A'' → 0, the image Φ(A) → Φ(A') → Φ(A'') → 0 fails exactness. **Derived functors** L¹Φ and R¹Φ then **quantify** emergence—measuring how far operations deviate from exactness.

William Lawvere's categorical formalization of Hegelian dialectics provides additional machinery. **Adjoint functors** constitute the mathematical form of "unity of opposites":

- **Left adjoint F**: "thesis" (free construction, synthesis)
- **Right adjoint G**: "antithesis" (forgetful, analytic)  
- **Adjunction relationship**: "synthesis" (Aufhebung)—the least level simultaneously preserving essential structure from both directions

The counit ε: FG → 1 and unit η: 1 → GF satisfying triangle identities formalize how thesis and antithesis produce synthesis while preserving their essential contributions.

**Convexity** provides another emergence criterion relevant to GIT. Convex regions exhibit compositional, predictable behavior—any local optimum is global, separation theorems apply. **Non-convex** regions exhibit multiple local optima, potential instability, market failures in economics, and emergent complexity. The convex hull operator functions as a "grounding" operation returning systems to compositional domains.

**Persistent homology** detects emergent topological features across scales: constructing simplicial complexes at varying parameters ε, tracking birth and death of homology classes (connected components, loops, voids), and identifying features persisting across many scales as "genuine" emergent structure versus short-lived noise.

## The combination problem and geometric solutions

The **combination problem** (Chalmers, 2017) asks how micro-level conscious experiences combine into macro-level consciousness. Sub-problems include subject-summing (how multiple subjects compose into one), quality combination (how micro-qualities combine into macro-qualities), and structural combination (how micro-structure yields macro-phenomenal structure).

IIT provides a principled geometric approach through two key postulates:

1. **Integration postulate**: Only irreducible systems (φ > 0) are conscious—parts that don't integrate don't separately contribute consciousness
2. **Exclusion postulate**: Only the **maximally** integrated system (φ*) exists consciously—nested or overlapping systems don't multiply consciousness

The Φ-structure emerges from compositional structure of distinctions and relations, with the exclusion postulate establishing definite "boundaries" analogous to polytope faces. Giulio Tononi's IIT "can be construed as a form of emergent panpsychism" (Chalmers, 2017)—consciousness emerges from integration patterns rather than summing micro-experiences.

GIT proposes extending this through the 24-cell's tripartite structure:

- **Alpha and Beta 16-cells**: Represent established archetypal structures (dialectical poles, existing conceptual content)
- **Gamma 16-cell**: Represents "in-process-of-formation" prototype—concepts being formed through relation
- **Coaxial rotation**: Dynamic transformation where multiple 24-cell constellations generate emergent non-convex structure

The combination mechanism is geometric integration rather than simple summation—the triality structure ensures that combination preserves irreducibility while generating genuinely novel structural configurations.

> p(z₀, z₁) = (2z₀z̄₁, |z₀|² - |z₁|²)
